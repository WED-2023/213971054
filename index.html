Q2:answer

  • Web1: You would visit a static website with basic text-based information. Results may come from a medical encyclopedia or government health page. The content is not interactive and cannot be personalized.
  • Web2: You would search on a platform like Google or Wikipedia, and you would see dynamic, user-generated content like forums, articles, and reviews. The search results would be personalized based on your previous browsing habits and profile, but the data is controlled by centralized entities.
  • Web3: You would access a decentralized medical dApp that pulls information from a distributed network of verified contributors.
